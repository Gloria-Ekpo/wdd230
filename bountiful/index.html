Information about our company

Bountiful Foods

Welcome to Bountiful Foods! We're a small business based in Los Angeles, California that's passionate about bringing you the freshest and healthiest food options. Our food trucks cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, so whether you're a local or a tourist, you can enjoy our delicious organic, locally grown foods. We source our fruits and produce directly from local farmers, as well as some national and international suppliers, to bring you the best of both worlds. Our made-to-order organic fruit drinks and fruit bags are perfect for a quick and healthy snack on the go. We cater to walk-in traffic at our storefront in Los Angeles, and we also manage phone and website orders for delivery. Our mission is to provide you with fresh and healthy food options that support your healthy lifestyle, while also making it convenient for you to enjoy them. So come on down to our storefront or find us on the road with our food trucks! We can't wait to share our love for organic, locally grown foods with you.
