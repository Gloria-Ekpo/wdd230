Site Name: Calabar Chamber of Commerce

The name represents the city of Calabar, which is the capital city of Cross River State, Nigeria. It's a city rich in history and culture, making it an ideal location for chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

  • To promote the business interests of the members of the Calabar Chamber of Commerce.
  • To attract additional business, tourism, and people to Calabar by showcasing its rich history, culture, and economic opportunities.

Scenerios

  • What events will the Calabar Chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Calabar Chamber's board of directors?
  • What are the economic growth trends in Calabar?

Color Schema

  • Primary color: #008000 (Green), to be used for headings and important text. It represents the lush vegetation and natural beauty of Calabar.
  • Secondary color: #0000FF (Blue), to be used for paragrahs and general text. This represents the Calabar and Great Kwa rivers that are adjacent to the city.

Typography

  • Heading font: Arial, sans-serif.
  • Body font: Times New Roman, serif
