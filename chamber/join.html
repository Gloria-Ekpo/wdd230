Membership Level

Membership Levels Benefits

NP Membership

  • No Fee: As a non-profit, no fee is associated with this membership level.
  • Basic Access to Resources: Access to basic resources and information offered by the organization.

Bronze Membership

  • Cost: $100: Membership at the Bronze level costs $100.
  • Event Discounts: Discounts for attending organization-sponsored events, workshops, or seminars.
  • Basic Advertising Opportunities: Basic advertising or promotional opportunities in the organization's materials.

Silver Membership

  • Cost: $250: Membership at the Silver level costs $250.
  • Enhanced Event Discounts: Higher discounts for attending events compared to the Bronze level.
  • Enhanced Advertising Opportunities: More prominent advertising space in the organization's materials.
  • Exclusive Training Sessions: Access to exclusive training or educational sessions.

Gold Membership

  • Cost: $500: Membership at the Gold level costs $500.
  • Premier Event Discounts: The highest discounts for attending events.
  • VIP Access to Special Events: Access to exclusive VIP events or gatherings.
  • Premier Advertising Opportunities: Priority placement for advertising in the organization's materials.
  • Exclusive Training Sessions: Advanced and specialized training sessions tailored for Gold members.
