Economy

Calabar's economy is diverse and robust, with key sectors including tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing.

The city is also home to the Calabar Free Trade Zone, attracting businesses from around the globe.

Attractions

From the historical Slave Museum to the beautiful Marina Resort, there's something for everyone in Calabar.

Natur lovers can explore the National Park, while those interested in culture can visit the Cultural Center Complex.

Events

Calabar is known for its lively events calender. The highlight of the year is the Calabar Carnival attracting visitors from around the world.

Other events include the Boat Regatta and the Ekpe Festival
